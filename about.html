Protect Endangered Species

Our mission


Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of biodiversity and the organisms that live on our planet. Humans have been killing species and damaging their habitats. We work with World Wildlife Fund, U.S. Fish & Wildlife Services, Endangered Species Coalition and Environmental Protection Agency to ensure that these species are protected properly.


How you can help


Everyone can help make a difference in their own way. Here is a list of some things you should consider:


  1. Donate
  2. Reduce climate change
  3. Buy sustainable products
  4. Protect wildlife habitats
  5. Volunteer
  6. Fundraise
  7. Spread the word
